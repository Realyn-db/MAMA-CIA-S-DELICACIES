Benefits: It provides quick energy because of its carbohydrates and sugar content. The eggs and butter also give small amounts of protein and healthy fats that help the body stay nourished.

Product Feature: Tinubaan Torta

Description: Tinubaan Torta is a soft, rich, and slightly sweet Filipino cake made with flour, eggs, sugar, and tuba (coconut wine) as a natural leavening ingredient. It has a unique aroma and flavor that makes it different from regular torta.

Benefits: It gives quick energy from its carbohydrates and sugar content. The eggs provide protein and healthy fats that help support body growth and repair.

Advantages: Tinubaan Torta has a unique taste that attracts customers looking for traditional Filipino flavors. It is also easy to store, lasts long, and is perfect for snacks, gifts, or celebrations.

Nutritional Value: Tinubaan Torta provides carbohydrates for energy, fats for fullness, and protein from eggs. It also offers Vitamin A and Vitamin E (from butter), and B vitamins such as B2, B6, B12, thiamine, and niacin from eggs and flour.

Product Feature: Fruit Salad

Description: Fruit salad is a sweet and creamy dessert made from mixed fruits like pineapple, apple, grapes, and nata de coco, usually combined with cream and condensed milk. It is cold, refreshing, and perfect for celebrations or family gatherings.

Benefits: Fruit salad provides natural vitamins and antioxidants from fresh fruits that help boost the immune system. It also offers fiber, which supports healthy digestion.

Advantages: Fruit salad is easy to prepare and can be customized with different fruits based on availability and preference. It is also affordable and enjoyed by people of all ages, making it ideal for parties and special occasions.

Nutritional Value: Fruit salad contains vitamin C, vitamin A, fiber, and potassium from fruits. The cream and milk add calcium and energy through carbohydrates and healthy fats.
